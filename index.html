The Eras Tour

The Eras Tour was the sixth concert tour by Taylor Swift. It began in
Glendale,Arizona,United States, on March 17, 2023. And the Eras Tour became the
highest-grossing tour of all time and the first to earn over $1 billion and $2 billion
in revenue.


Swift designed the tour as a retrospective tribute to all of her
studio albums.Running over 3.5 hours, the set list consisted of over 40 songs.
As of now, Toylor swift has released a total of 12 studio albums.
